Welcome to Temp Jobs, Nigerian Job site.



Vision

We are poised to becoming the first one stop employment platform with an excellent uniform standard in offering secondary school leaver employment opportunities.


Mission

We are committed to provide an open opportunity for secondary school leaver by granting access to the best outsourced job resource.


Overview

Temp Jobs is a website for connecting secondary school leavers seeking for temporary jobs and companies who wants to hire temporary workers. The site aims to revolutionize Nigerian youth to get them engaged thereby giving productivity to themselves and the nation as a whole through job opportunities that will be available on our site.

Temp Jobs is a jobsite platform that will promote business contacts. Services we will be offering will enable Nigerian companies to develop partnerships, to find investors, to be known professionals and identify them. To do this, users have the option of completing online resumes, company profiles and offers of collaboration.

Our site will ensure that companies (employers) conduct their recruitment and online job seekers maximize their opportunities. Job seekers will be able to select jobs from sections of interest.

For credibility, we will ensure that the rights of both the employer and the job seeker will be ensured. We will offer a rating service by employers and a token from job seekers. This allows for smooth running and an attraction for partnership or sponsorship.

Temp Jobs is a website whose rights is solely for TECHIES GROUP with a team of Four.
